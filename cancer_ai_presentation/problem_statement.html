Problem Statement & Motivation

Cancer Research Challenges

Cancer remains a leading cause of death globally, with complex biological mechanisms that require sophisticated analytical approaches to understand and treat effectively.

Data Integration Complexity

Cancer research data exists across multiple repositories in various formats, making comprehensive analysis challenging and time-consuming.

Pattern Recognition Limitations

Traditional statistical methods may miss subtle patterns in high-dimensional genomic and clinical data that could be crucial for treatment decisions.

Manual Analysis Bottlenecks

Current research approaches often involve manual analysis of limited datasets, creating bottlenecks in discovering new insights and therapeutic targets.
